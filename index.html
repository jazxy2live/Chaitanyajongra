I thought everybody puts out who they are, i want to show you who i am not, so here it is —

Who I Am NOT

01.

I'm not the type of person who enjoys copying or making something that exists already in the world and making it better, cheaper, faster. That's microeconomic shit. That's fucking globalization. It's not my thing. I don't want to make things that are filthy or ordinary.

02.

I'm not the person who chases fame or status that doesn't support who I am or my deeds. For power or control — that's totally another aspect.

03.

I'm not the type of person who is going to retire. First of all, this word means death to me. The moment I stop admiring my time and go to pee in the bushes, it's the time I feel I lost my spirit. And losing my spirit means death. I don't want to die. I mean literally.

04.

I'm not the person who sits and waits for the future to unfold. I want to be part of the future in some or the other way. And I damn don't mean small in any other goddamn way.
